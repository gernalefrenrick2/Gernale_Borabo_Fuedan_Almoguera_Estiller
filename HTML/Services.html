Services of Sean Production

Wedding Photography/Videography

Professional photographers and videographers are experts at capturing the priceless moments and memories of a wedding day. They use their knowledge of composition, lighting, and storytelling to produce visually amazing renditions of the event. They may provide packages that include pre-wedding shoots, ceremony and reception coverage, as well as editing and delivery of final images or movies.

Wedding Planning/Coordination

Wedding planners or coordinators assist couples in planning and organizing their wedding from start to end. They help with budgeting, vendor selection, venue coordination, timetable management, and event logistics in general. Wedding planners can help relieve the burden of wedding planning by ensuring that everything runs smoothly and in accordance with the couple's vision.

Wedding Catering

Wedding caterers are experts at providing delectable food and beverages for wedding receptions. They provide a variety of menu selections, ranging from buffet-style meals to plated dinners, and can accommodate a variety of dietary restrictions or preferences. Caterers handle the food and beverage station setup, serving, and cleanup, allowing the couple and their guests to enjoy a wonderful dining experience.
